Welcome to the Vodafone Resource Centre

On this site you'll find everything you need to know about the MachineLink family created exclusively for Vodafone by NetComm Wireless.

Device images, specification sheets, videos and more can be found on the individual product pages and new resources and products will be added as they become available.

NOTICE

Vodafone wishes to advise its customers and partners that the MachineLink 3G, MachineLink 3G Plus and MachineLink 4G have been withdrawn from sale as of December 2020. Critical bug fixes and other vital updates will continue for a further 3 years until December 2023, after which all technical support will be withdrawn.

Vodafone recommends the MachineLink 4G Lite as a replacement product going forward.

NOTICE

Vodafone wishes to advise its customers and partners that the MachineLink Ethernet Switch has been withdrawn from sale as of January 2021. Technical support will continue for a further 3 years until January 2024.
